From Austin to BYU

I began my journey in the vibrant city of Austin, Texas, where I was taught the value of hard work, perseverance, and a sense of community. Moving from Austin to Brigham Young University (BYU) was a significant transition, but it opened up new opportunities for growth both academically and personally. At BYU, I not only immersed myself in my studies but also explored various extracurricular activities that expanded my horizons and helped me develop valuable skills.

Passion for Learning and Basketball

One of the driving forces behind my success is my passion for learning. Whether in the classroom or through personal interests, I am always seeking new ways to improve and expand my knowledge. This passion extends beyond academics to my love for basketball. The lessons I’ve learned from sports—teamwork, discipline, and perseverance—have had a profound impact on my personal and academic achievements.
